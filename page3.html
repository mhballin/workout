SHIT you should know about this plan. 


Rep Ranges: 4x4-6: This means you are doin 4 sets, and between 4-6 reps. The reason there is a rep range is so that you can more esialy progress without failing a lot (not finishing a rep). So lets say you are benching, do 6 reps for 4 sets. Next time you bench, you will add 5 to 10 pounds. Increasing the weight when you hit the max rep range. 

SS This means its a superset, you do one set on one excerisies (ex. Bench dips), then go on to do one set on the next excrise (ex Dumbbell Scaption raise). After that rest, then, go back to the first excrises and do the second set, and so on. 

*So for wide grip pulls ups, you will not be able to do 8-10 (i cant even do that shit). So you could start with bands under your feet, or just do super low reps.
